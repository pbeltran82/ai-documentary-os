# Creator Pain Log

This file records observed production friction before proposing features. Product priorities should come from repeated, real workflow pain.

## 2026-07-14 — Financial and investment Short

### Pain 1: Finding suitable images and video for each narration segment

**Observed workflow**

- The script and voiceover were already usable.
- Each narration segment required a separate visual idea and search.
- Pexels searches often required several keyword variations and significant scrolling.
- A 60-second Short using five-second visuals required roughly 12 assets.
- An eight-minute documentary at the same pace could require roughly 96 assets.

**Why it hurts**

The creator repeatedly translates narration into keywords, searches, previews, rejects, downloads, renames, and organizes files. This scales poorly with video length.

**Potential product response**

- Create timed scenes from narration.
- Generate multiple stock-media search queries per scene.
- Search providers inside the app.
- Attach the selected asset directly to the scene.
- Suggest AI generation only when stock media is weak or inappropriate.

### Pain 2: Manually assembling assets into timeline slots

**Observed workflow**

- Assets were dragged into CapCut one by one.
- Each asset required trimming and alignment to the narration.
- Repeating this for even a one-minute Short was tedious.

**Why it hurts**

The work is repetitive bookkeeping rather than creative editing. The narration timing already contains most of the information needed to place and trim assets.

**Potential product response**

- Store scene start, end, and duration.
- Attach one or more assets to each scene.
- Automatically create a first-pass timeline.
- Render a review copy with FFmpeg.
- Leave pacing, transitions, emphasis, and final polish to the creator.

## Low-friction steps observed

- AI-assisted script creation was acceptable.
- Voice generation in Google AI Studio was manageable.
- YouTube upload was manageable.

These areas should not receive priority over asset discovery and timeline assembly unless future workflow evidence changes the ranking.
