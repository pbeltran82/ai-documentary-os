## 21. Sensitivity and Ethics Review

- Avoid presenting allegations as established facts
- Distinguish evidence from interpretation
- Include relevant context and competing accounts
- Avoid unnecessary graphic detail
- Protect private individuals where appropriate
- Flag legal or reputational risks before publication

## 22. Research Completion Checklist

- [ ] Thesis supported by evidence
- [ ] Timeline completed

## 23. Handoff to Scriptwriting

Before scriptwriting begins, produce:

1. A one-paragraph story summary
2. A one-sentence thesis
3. A complete chronological timeline
4. A list of verified facts with citations
5. A list of unresolved or disputed claims
6. A recommended three-act structure
7. A visual asset shortlist
8. A source bibliography
